My ideas were not manufactured in boardrooms.
They were lived.

My frameworks were built everywhere from parking lots in Northeast Ohio to remote temples in Thailand's mountains I quite literally stumbled upon while traveling solo across the world.

They were formed while living inside the gaps most individuals and organizations ignore.

  • The ongoing battle between Marketing and Sales.
  • The disconnect between Acquisition and Retention.
  • The space between promises made and what customers actually experience.
  • The emotional and operational damage caused when leadership and trust fall out of alignment.
  • What people say publicly versus how they actually behave once pressure, incentives, and uncertainty enter the room.

I've spent the last 15 years sitting in the middle of that chaos trying to reconnect the pieces nobody else wanted to own.

My experiences changed how I see business. And even more so, how I see people.

Over time, I started noticing the same pattern everywhere. Most people are not failing because they lack talent, intelligence, or effort. They're struggling because there's a disconnect somewhere. Between who they are, what they offer, and how the world experiences them.

I call them The GAPS™.

That realization became the foundation for The Cosmic Change Agency. Today, I help businesses and individuals identify The GAPS™ preventing them from sustainable growth.

I can help. And I will.

I write regularly through my #PayAttention thought series, where I decode AI's impact on everything before most people fully realize what's changing.
